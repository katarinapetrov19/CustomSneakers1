About the Artist

Hi, I'm Sebastian Wagner, a passionate custom sneaker artist and mural designer based in the heart of the city. With over 5 years of experience in transforming everyday footwear into unique pieces of wearable art, I specialize in hand-painted designs that tell a story.

My work combines street art aesthetics with fine art techniques, creating bold and vibrant designs that stand out. From custom sneakers to large-scale murals, each project is an opportunity to push creative boundaries and bring imagination to life.

I believe that art should be accessible and personal. Whether it's a pair of sneakers that reflects your personality or a mural that transforms a space, I'm dedicated to creating pieces that inspire and connect with people.
